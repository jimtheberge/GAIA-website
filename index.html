Response mode
How autonomous should Aria be?
Fully autonomous
Aria replies instantly to all customer messages without review
Assisted mode
Aria drafts replies, your team approves before sending. Good for high-stakes businesses.
Monitoring only
Aria observes and flags conversations. Your team handles all replies.
